Full job description

We're looking for a passionate Data Scientist with an entrepreneurial spirit to join the Technology Innovation team in Toronto. You are driven and resourceful, capable of building data-driven insights that address complex current and future business problems. In this role, you will demonstrate the art of the possible and have a significant and direct impact on our company's products.

Our team’s goal is to apply AI/ML technologies, like NLP, Computer Vision, Generative Language, and Vision models, to the plethora of data (videos, pictures, text, and audio) created by Reuters News editors to streamline content generation processes and support creation of new B2B and B2C client-facing products. We are looking for data scientists excited to work with an abundance of text data in collaboration with other specialists, applying state-of-the-art models to create innovative solutions to challenging business problems.

We have data. Lots and lots of data. Over 60,000 TBs worth of data. That's about 20,000 times more data than exists in Wikipedia.

Come play with us.

Build new solutions from the ground up, working like a startup, utilizing the resources of a large enterprise.

Qualifications

Experience in one or more of,

  • Information retrieval or data mining

  • NLP, Computer Vision, Generative AI

  • Machine learning

  • Preprocessing, cleaning, and augmentation of unstructured data (images, video, audio, text, etc)

  • Experience producing and rapidly delivering minimum viable products

  • Experience with programming languages like Python, Java, or Scala

  • Experience with relational, NoSQL, or graph databases


Bonus:

  • Big data analytics or visualization

  • Hadoop, Hive, NoSQL, Spark, Shark, Hive, Mahout, Impala, Solr, HBase, Pig, Cascading, Elastic Search, Tableau, D3

Learning & Development: LinkedIn Learning access; internal Talent Marketplace with opportunities to work on projects cross-company; Ten Thousand Coffees Thomson Reuters café networking.

Social Impact: Ten employee-driven Business Resource Groups; two paid volunteer days annually; Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) initiatives for local and global impact.

Purpose-Driven Work: We have a superpower that we’ve never talked about with as much pride as we should – we are one of the only companies on the planet that helps its customers pursue justice, truth, and transparency. Together, with the professionals and institutions we serve, we help uphold the rule of law, turn the wheels of commerce, catch bad actors, report the facts, and provide trusted, unbiased information to people all over the world.
